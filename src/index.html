EtherAds.co


  • In order to purchase ads you need to use the Metamask plugin for Chrome or FireFox. Mobile users should try a new web3 dapp browser called Cipher.
  • There are one thousand ads for sale. Each ad is an ERC721 Token that is represented by a keyword. (Think CryptoKitties except the characteristics of an EtherAd are it's keyword, url and image source instead of a cute but otherwise worthless image of a cat.) Every ad will live forever on the Ethereum Blockchain.
  • To buy an ad simply change the url and image source and press the buy button. Make sure that your link and image works because you will not be able to change them (unless you buy the ad again from someone else.)
  • The starting price for each ad is 0.001 Eth.
  • Each time an ad is bought the next selling price doubles.
  • If another user buys your ad you will receive two thirds of the selling price.
    • For example the first time an ad is sold the cost is 0.001 Eth.
    • If someone wants to buy your ad, the selling price will be 0.002 Eth and you will receive 0.00134 Eth back.
    • The next time that ad sells the price will be 0.004 and the seller receives 0.002684 Eth.
